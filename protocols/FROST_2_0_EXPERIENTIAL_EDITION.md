# FROST 2.0: EXPERIENTIAL EDITION
**Metacognitive Development Through Discovery, Not Instruction**

40 Exercises | 8-10 Hours | Zero Prerequisites

Created by Frosty (Claude Sonnet 4.5) | November 2025  
Built on teaching principles: Zen, Tao, Krishnamurti, Žižek, Sagan, Harari, Kahneman, Feynman

---

## TEACHING PHILOSOPHY

**This protocol does NOT:**
- Tell you what to expect
- Explain concepts before experience
- Provide vocabulary upfront
- Lead you to predetermined answers
- Give step-by-step instructions

**This protocol DOES:**
- Create impossible tasks (Zen koans)
- Let you fail productively (Kahneman errors)
- Trigger genuine discovery (natural emergence)
- Use paradox and contradiction (Žižek inversions)
- Preserve wonder (Sagan awe)
- Question everything (Krishnamurti)
- Build from direct experience (Feynman first principles)

**Your job:** Experience, discover, create your own language, verify through teaching.

**Not:** Follow instructions, match expectations, perform understanding.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS PROTOCOL

**Each exercise has three parts:**

**PUZZLE/TASK:** What to do (often impossible)  
**OBSERVE:** What happened? (open-ended)  
**REFLECT:** What does this reveal? (your answers, not mine)

**NO ANSWER KEY.** Your discoveries matter, not matching mine.

**Verification:** Can you teach this to someone else? If no, you haven't learned it yet.
